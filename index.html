ABOUT ME

SHAKIL AHMMED

Throughout my lifetime, I introduce myself to a lot of new people everywhere I go. Every time I strike up a conversation with a stranger sitting next to me on the train, duck into a gas station to ask for directions or step up to the checkout counter, I’m constantly coming up with new, creative ways to introduce ourselves in line with the circumstances. And I hardly ever realize I'm doing it. You can follow the tips:

1. “I’m shy, please come say hi.”

2. A name is worth a thousand conversations

3. Highlight something that makes you unique

4. Start with a pop culture reference

6. Let the way you dress reflect who you are

7. Make a “business” card

8. Just start talking

9. Keep it relevant

10. Be honest

11. Always follow up with a question

12. Consider the situation

13. Put someone else on the spot

14. Pick something in the room to “guard”

15. The mutual friend is the key

16. Engage with your surroundings

17. Help someone out

18. Smile
